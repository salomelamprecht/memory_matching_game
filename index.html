Memory Matching Game

Instructions

Select your required theme and click on Start Game. Once the game has started pick two cards, if the cards match, pick another two cards until you have matched all the cards. If your cards do not match, continue selecting cards until they match. If you would like to restart your game, click restart or if you want to quit the game, select quit.

Enjoy your game!
